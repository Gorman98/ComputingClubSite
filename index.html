Announcements

  • The Computing Club had the annual hackathon last month, and it was a huge success. Classrooms were full of hardworking eager students who wanted to win or even place. The competition was tight for the top positions, as 3 great politcal tools/applications won. Congratulations to all of the winners. The next hackathon will take place in the Spring, so get excited!

  • If you are interested in the computing club, come to a meeting! meetings are held biweekly on tuesday nights after classes, at 9:15. You can apply online to be apart of the club. Don't be shy, come on by!

  • Chick-Fil-A and Dunkin Donuts have partnered with the computing club to provide discounted snacks for the computing club. This food will be available at all events and meetings from now on.
