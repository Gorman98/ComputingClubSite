Our Mission:

To give the students at Quinnipiac a community to improve their technical skills!

History

Established when Quinnipiacs School of Engineering was established. The computing club was established as a faculty led club that aimed to help students in the computer science or software engineering program. Later in its life, it was extended to anyone who was interested in computing. The club now, is student run by four executive board members who elect those individuals every year. The club is primarily for undergraduates, however graduates and alumni are always welcome as the computing club, and the overall Quinnipiac Engineering program, are an inclusive community with a close culture.

What we do

We enjoying a range of events, which can be found here.

  • Hackathons
  • Coding tutorials
  • Barbecues
  • Hacking tutorials
  • Homework Help!
